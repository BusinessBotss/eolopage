Eolomarine Group comercializa las históricas embarcaciones Eolo desde 1971. En 2013 creó una joint venture con AS Marine Italia para mejorar red comercial, gama y control de calidad.

Hoy el grupo ofrece más de 20 modelos de 5,00 m a 8,30 m en versiones Open, Day, Cabin, Walk Around y Fishing, con sede en Sant'Agata di Militello (Messina).

News & Events

Eolomarine Group

Eolomarine Group sells the historic Eolo boats, active in the Italian and European nautical market since 1971. In 2013, the group created a joint venture with AS Marine Italia to strengthen sales, model range and quality control.

Today the group offers more than 20 models, from 5.00 m to 8.30 m, in Open, Day, Cabin, Walk Around and Fishing versions, with production and operations based in Sant'Agata di Militello (Messina).
